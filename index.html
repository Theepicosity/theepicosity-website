Hi! My name is Theepicosity, also known as kule epiku Atawan or by my real name, Adrian. After all these years, I finally have a website!
Variations on my name include: TheepicosityEpic, k.e. Atawan, theepie, or (for short) theep, epic, atawan, kule epiku, adri, and osi.

Click on one of the tabs to learn more about me and my website!
I'm 19 years old, born on March 4th. (pisces!) I live in Phoenix, Arizona. (GMT-7) I often use a lizard profile picture, even though I have three pet rats. Music composition is my main hobby, and I enjoy making games and writing too. I work as a library page. I am transgender (she/her), biracial (black & white), and I look like this!
